About

FauxScopes is a simple application to compute your actual Astrological sign.

Due to the fact that the people who invented Horoscopes were too stupid to understand the astronomical procession of the stars, what you believe your actual horoscope to be is anything but. This calculator will compute your actual sign so that you can still be uninformed about your future.
